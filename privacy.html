# Privacy Policy **App:** DFUCapp — Diabetic Foot Ulcer Screening **Developer:** Tran Ngoc Hoang Phap **Last Updated:** April 8, 2026 **Effective Date:** April 8, 2026 --- ## 1. Introduction This Privacy Policy explains how DFUCapp ("the App", "we", "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when healthcare professionals use the App to screen and document diabetic foot conditions. DFUCapp is designed exclusively for licensed healthcare professionals — doctors, nurses, and clinical staff. It is not intended for use by patients or the general public. We are committed to protecting patient privacy and complying with applicable data protection laws, including Vietnam's Personal Data Protection Decree (PDPD 2023), Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA), and, where applicable, the European Union General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). --- ## 2. Who We Are **Developer / Data Controller:** Tran Ngoc Hoang Phap Operating as Lamedtech Contact: [dfucapp.support@lamedtech.org](mailto:dfucapp.support@lamedtech.org) --- ## 3. Information We Collect ### 3.1 Clinician Account Information When you register and use the App, we collect: - Full name and professional title - Email address - Hospital or clinic affiliation and department - Role (doctor, nurse, screener) ### 3.2 Patient Clinical Data When you perform a foot screening or follow-up visit, the App collects and stores: - Patient name, date of birth, and diabetes type - Foot photographs and close-up wound images - Clinical findings (monofilament test results, pulse checks, skin condition assessments) - Risk level assessment and screening notes - Referral information and follow-up records **This data is entered by the clinician on behalf of their patient. Patients do not interact with the App directly.** ### 3.3 Device and Usage Data We may collect limited technical information including: - App version and operating system version - Crash logs and error reports (used solely to improve App stability) - Session timestamps We do **not** collect advertising identifiers, precise location, or any data for tracking or marketing purposes. --- ## 4. How We Use Information | Data type | Purpose | |-----------|---------| | Clinician account data | Authentication, session management, audit trail | | Patient clinical data | Storing and displaying screening records within the App | | Foot photographs | Clinical documentation; AI-assisted risk assessment | | Usage / crash data | App stability and improvement | We do **not** sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for commercial purposes. We do not use patient data to train AI models without explicit written consent. --- ## 5. AI-Assisted Analysis The App includes an AI feature that analyses foot wound images and suggests a risk classification. This output is: - **Clinical decision support only** — it does not constitute a medical diagnosis - **Not a substitute** for professional clinical judgement - Reviewed and confirmed by the attending clinician before any clinical action is taken Image processing for AI analysis is performed on-device where possible. When server-side processing is required, images are transmitted securely over encrypted connections and are not retained by the AI service beyond the immediate analysis. --- ## 6. Data Storage and Cross-Border Transfer Patient data and clinician account data are stored on servers provided by **Supabase, Inc.**, located in **Singapore**. By using the App, you acknowledge and consent to the transfer of data to Singapore. Singapore is governed by the Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA), which provides data protection standards recognised as adequate under Vietnam's PDPD 2023 for cross-border transfer purposes. Data is transmitted using industry-standard TLS encryption. Data at rest is encrypted by Supabase's infrastructure. --- ## 7. Third-Party Service Providers | Provider | Role | Data processed | Location | |----------|------|----------------|----------| | Supabase, Inc. | Database and authentication infrastructure | All app data | Singapore | Supabase acts as a **data processor** under our instruction. They do not access or use your data for their own purposes. Supabase's privacy policy is available at [supabase.com/privacy](https://supabase.com/privacy). --- ## 8. Data Security We implement the following security measures: - **Biometric authentication** (Face ID / Touch ID) required to access the App - **Automatic lock** after configurable inactivity timeout - **Privacy overlay** that hides patient data in the iOS app switcher - **TLS encryption** for all data in transit - **Row-level security** on the database — clinicians can only access records they are authorised to view - **Role-based access control** — pending approval required before a new account can access patient data No security system is impenetrable. In the event of a data breach that affects your personal data, we will notify affected users as required by applicable law. --- ## 9. Data Retention | Data type | Retention period | |-----------|-----------------| | Patient screening records | Until deleted by the clinician or institution | | Clinician account data | Until account is deleted | | Crash / error logs | 90 days | You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting [dfucapp.support@lamedtech.org](mailto:dfucapp.support@lamedtech.org). --- ## 10.
